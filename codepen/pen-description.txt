TITLE:
Thermal Touch — Interactive Heat Diffusion Effect

DESCRIPTION:
An interactive canvas-based heat diffusion effect. Move your mouse (or finger on mobile) to paint heat that spreads, glows, and fades in real time. Choose from 6 color palettes including thermal, night vision, rainbow, and synthwave. Adjust brush size, hardness, glow radius, and burn rate with the control panel.

GitHub: https://github.com/kidhack/Thermal-Touch
Built by ABL — https://www.abl.design
